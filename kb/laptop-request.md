# Laptop Request and Replacement

New joiners receive a standard laptop on day one, arranged automatically by HR onboarding — no ticket needed.

Replacement laptops are issued when: the device is more than 3 years old, has a hardware fault confirmed by IT, or is lost/stolen (file a security report first at security.internal). Battery degradation below 60% of design capacity counts as a hardware fault — check yours under Settings → Battery Health and attach a screenshot to your ticket.

To request a replacement, raise an IT ticket with your asset tag (sticker on the underside). Standard replacements ship within 5 working days to the office, or 7-10 to a home address.

Upgrades outside the 3-year cycle (more RAM, a dedicated GPU) require a business justification approved by your manager and are fulfilled subject to budget. Old devices must be returned within two weeks of receiving the replacement or the cost is charged to your department.
