# VPN Setup Guide

To connect to the company VPN, install the GlobalConnect client from the internal software portal (portal.internal/software). Sign in with your company email and your network password, then approve the push notification on your registered phone.

Common issues:

- **"Authentication failed"** usually means your network password expired. Reset it at password.internal first, wait five minutes, then try again.
- **"Gateway unreachable"** on hotel or airport Wi-Fi: switch the client protocol from UDP to TCP in Settings → Connection.
- If the client connects but internal sites do not load, disconnect, clear the client cache (Settings → Advanced → Clear Cache), and reconnect.

The VPN session lasts 12 hours, after which you must re-authenticate. If you are still unable to connect after these steps, raise an IT ticket and include the error code shown in the client window.
