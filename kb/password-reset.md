# Password Reset Policy

Network passwords expire every 90 days. You will receive email reminders starting 14 days before expiry.

To reset your password yourself, go to password.internal from any device on the office network or VPN, verify with your registered phone, and choose a new password. Passwords must be at least 12 characters with one number and one symbol, and cannot match any of your last five passwords.

If you are locked out (five failed attempts), the lock clears automatically after 30 minutes. If you cannot wait, call the IT hotline at extension 4357 — self-service reset does not work while an account is locked.

After a reset, allow up to 10 minutes for the new password to sync to email, VPN, and Wi-Fi. You will need to re-enter it on every device, including your phone's mail app, otherwise repeated failed syncs can lock the account again.
