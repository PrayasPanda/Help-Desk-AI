# Leave Application Process

All leave is requested through the HR portal (hr.internal → My Leave). Annual leave should be requested at least five working days in advance; your manager approves or declines within two working days.

Leave types and entitlements:

- **Annual leave**: 24 days per calendar year, up to 5 unused days carry over to March 31 of the following year.
- **Sick leave**: 10 days per year. For absences longer than 2 consecutive days, upload a medical certificate within one week of returning.
- **Casual leave**: 6 days per year, can be requested same-day before 10:00 AM.

Your live leave balance is shown on the portal dashboard. If a request has been pending more than two working days, the portal lets you send a reminder to your manager. Leave requests for a past date (regularization) must be applied within 15 days and require HR approval in addition to your manager's.
