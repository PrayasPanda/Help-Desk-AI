# Expense Reimbursement

Submit expense claims through the Finance portal (finance.internal → Claims) within 30 days of incurring the expense. Claims older than 30 days need written approval from your department head.

Every claim needs an itemized receipt — card statements alone are not accepted. Allowed categories: travel, client meals (up to $50 per person), software subscriptions pre-approved by IT, and office supplies for remote workers (up to $100 per quarter).

Approved claims are paid with the next payroll run. Claims submitted before the 15th of the month are processed in the same month; later submissions roll to the next cycle.

If a claim is rejected, the rejection email lists the reason, and you can fix and resubmit the same claim once without it counting as a new 30-day window. For questions about a specific claim, quote the claim ID (starts with EXP-) in your ticket.
